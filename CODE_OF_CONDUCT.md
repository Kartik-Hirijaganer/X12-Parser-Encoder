# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants in this project are expected to be direct, respectful, and technically honest. That includes:

- focusing criticism on ideas and code, not people
- assuming good intent while still challenging weak reasoning
- avoiding harassment, threats, discrimination, and personal attacks
- protecting private or sensitive data shared during collaboration

## Unacceptable Behavior

- abusive or insulting language
- discriminatory remarks
- doxxing or disclosure of private information
- deliberate disruption of review or discussion threads
- knowingly submitting real PHI or secrets into the repository

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior violates this policy.
